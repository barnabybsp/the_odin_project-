Creamy Chicken Pasta

Back to Recipes

Description

This creamy chicken pasta is a delicious and comforting dish that's perfect for any night of the week. Made with tender chicken, fresh spinach, and sun-dried tomatoes, it's a flavorful meal that's sure to please the whole family.
The creamy sauce is made with a blend of cream, garlic, and Parmesan cheese, creating a rich and indulgent flavor that pairs perfectly with the pasta. This dish is easy to make and can be ready in under 30 minutes, making it a great option for busy weeknights or when you want a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 8 ounces pasta of your choice (such as penne or fettuccine)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a simmer. Let it cook for a few minutes until it starts to thicken slightly.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and the sauce becomes creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the chopped spinach and sun-dried tomatoes to the skillet, and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the spinach wilts.
  7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and stir to combine everything. Let it cook for another minute to heat through.
  8. Finally, add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until the pasta is well coated with the creamy sauce. Serve immediately and enjoy!
